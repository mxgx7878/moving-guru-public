For Instructors

Your journey in
four steps

Getting started on Moving Guru is simple. Whether you're an experienced instructor or just beginning your global teaching journey, here's how it works.

01

Create Your Profile

Sign up, add your disciplines, languages, travel plans, and availability. Upload photos and tell the community about yourself.

02

Search & Discover

Browse instructors and studios worldwide. Filter by location, discipline, dates, and languages to find your perfect match.

03

Connect Directly

Message studios and instructors peer-to-peer. No middleman. Discuss swaps, placements, and collaborations on your terms.

04

Move & Teach

Organise your visa, flights, and accommodation. Travel the world doing what you love — teaching movement.

About Us

Built by movers, for movers

Moving Guru was born from a simple idea: talented wellness instructors shouldn't be limited by geography. Whether you're a Pilates teacher dreaming of Bali, a yoga instructor curious about Berlin, or a boxing coach ready for Buenos Aires — this platform is your launchpad.

We believe in direct, honest connections. No recruitment agencies taking a cut. No corporate middlemen. Just real people helping each other find incredible teaching opportunities around the world.

For Studios

Are you a studio looking for skilled substitutes or full-time staff?

Create your studio profile now and always find a Guru to fill in the roster. We have members from all over the world ready to share their talents and passions with your clients.

Your instructor is going away for a month? Find their substitute here at Moving Guru. Whether you need someone for a week or a full season, our network of passionate, qualified instructors is ready to step in and keep your classes running.

Two Paths

Swap or get
hired directly

Whether you want to exchange positions with another instructor or get hired directly by a studio overseas — Moving Guru makes it happen. No recruitment fees. No middlemen.

Swap

Exchange positions with an instructor in another country. You teach at their studio, they teach at yours.

Direct Hire

Get hired directly by a studio abroad. Work on their terms, explore a new city, grow your career.
